About us

We are born as the alternative to traditional Law Firms, our Lawyers are dedicated only to specific topics such as: Family Law; Labor Law and Intellectual Property.

We are committed to specialization, this being the key to strengthening and distinguishing our practice. Aspects such as the constant evolution of society, changes in requirements and needs, technological advances, economic situation, among others have justified customized solutions in a more personal environment.

Among the great advantages that a Boutique Law Firm can offer are: the flexibility that exists in terms of decision-making & billing and collection schemes. This adaptability allows the fees to fit the budget needs of our clients, in turn, providing a personalized service with high quality standards, where the personal relationship with the customer is always a priority.

Due to the structure of our Law Firm, hiring a Attorney is simpler and more direct compared to traditional Firms, this allows clients to deal directly with our legal professionals.

We are a team passionate for justice, with experience, a culture of entrepreneurship, always ready to offer quality services, innovative solutions, flexibility, confidence and confidentiality with the aim of giving the best legal advice to our clients.
